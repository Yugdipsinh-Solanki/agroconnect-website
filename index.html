About AgroConnect

AgroConnect is a platform designed to support farmers by connecting them directly with customers and local markets. It helps farmers get fair prices for their crops while providing important information such as market rates, government schemes, and agricultural guidance. Our goal is to strengthen rural agriculture through technology and awareness.
