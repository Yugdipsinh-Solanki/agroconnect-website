Government Agricultural Schemes

Explore various government initiatives designed to support farmers with financial aid, insurance, irrigation systems, and modern equipment.

Available Support Programs

Crop Insurance Program

Provides financial protection to farmers against crop failure due to natural disasters, pests, and diseases.

Covers up to 70% of crop loss compensation.

Irrigation Support Scheme

Offers subsidies for installing drip irrigation and sprinkler systems to improve water efficiency.

Up to 50% subsidy on irrigation equipment.

Farm Equipment Loan Assistance

Low-interest loans for purchasing tractors, harvesters, and modern agricultural tools.

Reduced interest rates for small and marginal farmers.

Seed & Fertilizer Subsidy

Financial assistance for purchasing high-quality seeds and fertilizers to improve crop yield.

Direct subsidy credited to farmer accounts.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Must be a registered farmer
  • Valid land ownership documents
  • Active bank account
  • Aadhaar or government ID proof
  • Registered in local agriculture office
